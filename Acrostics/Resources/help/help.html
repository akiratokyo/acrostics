Quick Start

  • Tap any cell to enter a letter - either in the top quotation area or in the clues. Related areas will update automatically.
  • If you've made a mistake or two, tap Undo (use the "@" key or select from the menu).
  • If you've made a real mess of it and want to start over, choose "Restart this Puzzle" from the Menu.

Choose your acrostic and start playing! The more stars, the more difficult it will be to solve a puzzle.

What is an Acrostic?

An acrostic puzzle is a hybrid between a cryptogram and a crossword puzzle. Your goal is to reveal a hidden quote in the grid at the top by correctly answering the crossword-style clues near the bottom of the puzzle. However, you can solve the quote and the clues simultaneously. Each letter in the quote is linked to a letter in one of the clue answers, so you can answer the clues or add to the quote.

As you answer more clues, more letters will begin to fill the quote grid. As you fill in the quote, parts of the clue answers are filled in. Eventually the entire quote is revealed.

What's more, the author and/or source of the quote will be spelled out by the first letter of each clue answer. This can also be used to help solve the puzzle.

Strategies

Acrostics are solved primarily by going back and forth between the different areas of the puzzle.

Clues are in the section right above the keyboard. Figuring out what word or phrase a clue is referring to will give you letters in both the quote and keywords.

Finishing a word that is almost complete in the quote will give you letters in clue solutions making it easier for you to figure out the answer to that clue.

When you have all the letters correct, the puzzle is complete!

Menu Items

The following menu options are available when appropriate:

  • Get a Hint - provide the answer for the next empty square. Hints are tallied and displayed on the completion screen.
  • Undo - undoes the last letter entered. You can undo repeatedly. To undo all the way back to the beginning try Restart this Puzzle.
  • Restart this Puzzle - clear all the letters to start again (does not reset the timer)
  • Erase Errors - clears all letters are that are incorrect. Any errors removed count as hints.
  • Solve this Puzzle - fill in the entire grid of the puzzle (does not count as solved)
  • Sound Effects - play or mute the sound effects
  • Erase All Player Records - completely resets the game to its original settings, clearing history and timings
